Privacy Policy

This policy explains what data ObsidianPluginIndex collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data. Last updated: 2026.

What data we collect

ObsidianPluginIndex collects minimal data. We do not require account creation, email signup, or any personally identifiable information to use the site.

We use Google Analytics to understand how the site is used in aggregate. This includes pages visited, time on site, general location (country level), device type, and browser. No individual users are tracked or identified. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by using the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

Cookies

This site uses cookies in two ways:

  • Analytics cookies. Google Analytics sets cookies to measure site usage in aggregate. These cookies do not identify you personally.
  • Advertising cookies. Google AdSense may set cookies to show relevant advertisements. These cookies are used by Google to serve ads based on your prior visits to this site and other sites on the internet.

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some site functionality and will change the ads you see, but will not prevent you from using the site.

Google AdSense

ObsidianPluginIndex uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense uses cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your interests. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to this site and other sites on the internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or by visiting aboutads.info.

For more information on how Google uses data when you use our site, visit How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps.

Third-party links

Plugin cards on this site link to the Obsidian community forums, GitHub repositories, and other external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We recommend reviewing the privacy policy of any external site you visit.

Data retention

We do not store personal data on our servers. Analytics data is retained by Google Analytics according to their data retention settings. We have configured a 14-month retention period.

Children

This site is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through this site, please contact us.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions about this privacy policy can be sent through our contact page.
