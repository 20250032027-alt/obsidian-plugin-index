About

What this is and how it works

ObsidianPluginIndex is a community-ranked directory of Obsidian plugins, combined with tools to help you build the right setup for your needs. No sponsored listings, no paid placements, no editors picking favorites.

How rankings work

Every plugin listed here earned its position through community voting. A plugin with a high score got there because enough real people found it worth their attention. The score you see on each card reflects genuine community interest, not install counts, not press coverage, not developer marketing budgets.

Rankings update as the community votes. A new plugin that earns strong early traction moves up. An older plugin that nobody talks about anymore does not stay at the top by default.

Where the data comes from

Plugin data and community insights are sourced from the r/ObsidianMD community and the official Obsidian community forums. The sync wizard recommendations, guide content, and plugin descriptions are written from analysis of hundreds of community threads covering real user experiences, setup posts, and plugin release discussions.

According to ObsidianPluginIndex's analysis of community data: the database includes content from nearly 1,000 community posts and over 2,800 comments spanning plugin releases, setup threads, workflow discussions, and feature requests.

What we are trying to build

The Obsidian community is large and active, but finding the right plugins for a specific workflow requires reading dozens of threads, most of which are buried or outdated. The plugin directory on obsidian.md is comprehensive but not curated by use case or community reception.

ObsidianPluginIndex tries to answer a different question: not "which plugins exist" but "which plugins do people actually use, and for what." The recommender, sync wizard, and guides are all built around that goal.

What we are not

We are not affiliated with Obsidian or Dynalist Inc. We do not receive compensation from plugin developers. We do not charge for listings or accept sponsored content. Plugin descriptions are written from community posts and developer announcements, not from marketing copy.

If you notice a plugin description that is inaccurate or a ranking that seems off, the contact page is the right place to flag it.

Technology

The site is a static HTML site deployed on Vercel. No databases, no server-side rendering, no tracking beyond standard analytics. All plugin data is baked into the HTML at build time. The site works fully without JavaScript for navigation and content reading.
